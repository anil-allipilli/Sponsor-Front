Users can register and apply for sponsorship. Applying for a sponsorship is a step by step
 process (wizards) that includes:

Collecting user bio-data (
  name, 
  address, 
  phone, 
  email, 
  Birth certificate file, 
  national id file
  )
School information (
  name, 
  address, 
  academic level of student, 
  expected year of completion
  )

Reasons they deserve to be sponsored and a recommendation letter.

Staff at the office are able to login and approve the application and the applicant is
 notified via email whether the application has been approved.

Sponsors are able to view approved applications and choose to sponsor the applicant.
 The applicant is notified once they are successfully sponsored with details of the
  sponsor included.
